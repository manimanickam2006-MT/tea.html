PREPARATION OF TEA

  1. Boil one cup of water in a saucepan on the stove.
  2. Add one teaspoon of tea leaves (or a tea bag) and sugar to taste.
  3. Let the mixture boil for 2–3 minutes until it releases color and aroma.
  4. Pour in a splash of milk and let it simmer for another minute, if desired.
  5. Strain the hot tea into a cup and enjoy.
